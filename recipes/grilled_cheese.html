Grilled Cheese

Description:

A recipe for grilled cheese, astoundingly easy to make and cheap!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Mayonaise
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Spread mayonaise on one side of bread, repeated for the other slice
  2. In a medium-heat frying pan, put one or your bread slices (mayonaise side down) into the pan
  3. Cut cheeses into proportionate chunks and place on top of bread in the pan
  4. Put other slice of bread on top of the cheese, once bottom of sandwhich is brown, flip
  5. Once both sides of toast are brwon and cheese is melted, turn stove off and enjoy!
